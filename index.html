Biography

Rick's backstory remained shrouded in mystery for much of the series. It wasn't until "Rickmurai Jack" where Rick's past is finally confirmed. The "totally fabricated" backstory shown in "The Rickshank Rickdemption" was partially based on the truth. Rick was once a scientist who was trying to invent teleportation. But one day, he was visited by an alternate version of himself, who offered him the ability to travel to different realities, but Rick refused. Angered by this, the other Rick killed Rick's wife, Diane, and his daughter, Beth. Rick grieved for several years, before inventing his own portal gun and going on a quest for revenge.
